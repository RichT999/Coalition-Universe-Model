# Postulate 1: Invariant Time Coordination

### The Concept
The Coalition Model replaces traditional relativistic "spacetime" variables with a fixed coordinate system. In this framework, time is treated as a medium-independent invariant.

### Key Assertions
* **Non-Dilation:** Time does not "dilate" due to velocity; rather, the medium's impedance ($Z$) slows down the physical clock mechanism itself.
* **Universal Clock:** All points in the universe share a synchronized temporal baseline, regardless of local gravitational density ($\rho$).
* **Computational Baseline:** Treating time as an invariant allows for a deterministic engineering approach to deep-space wave mechanics.

### Implications for the Model
By removing time as a variable, we can solve for $Z:\rho$ as a direct function of the medium's elastic limit ($\epsilon$).
